💻 Bot Commands Overview
🎁 Giftcard System
/get_conversion
View how many Diamonds are needed to convert into a gift card and what the value will be.

/convert_points
Convert your Diamonds into a gift card.

/convert_giftcard
Convert your gift card balance back into Diamonds.

/convert_currency
Simulate how much Diamonds are worth in different currencies. (Note: this does NOT convert your balance)

⏳ Daily Rewards
/claim_daily
Claim your daily reward every 24 hours.

Bonus: Claim within 36 hours to maintain your hidden streak. Streak rewards will be revealed soon!

💸 Points & Transfers
/get_points
Check how many Diamonds a user currently has.

/transfer_points
Transfer Diamonds to another user.

/get_multipliers
View your current earning multipliers based on activities or bonuses.

🎲 Mini Games
🪙 Coin Toss
Command: /coinflip

How to Play: Choose either Heads or Tails.

Reward: Win = +100 Diamonds

⚠️ Lose = No reward

🎯 Number Guessing
Command: /dice

How to Play: Guess the number the dice will land on (1 to 6).

Reward: Win = +100 Diamonds

❌ Incorrect = No reward
(Instructions shown when using the command)

📩 DM Checker
Command: /test_dm
Checks if the bot can send you Direct Messages (DMs).

🔐 This is required for claiming rewards and receiving updates!

🧩 Custom Game: ToS Coin Flip
/tos_coin
A special coin toss game based on Terms of Service Coin.

Pick either Head or Tail.

Win = +100 Diamonds

No Diamonds lost on failure.
